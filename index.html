28th IFFK Award Winners

Suvarna Chakoram for Best Film

Yoshio Kitagawa, DoP of Evil Does Not Exist receiving the Suvarna Chakoram for Best Film on behalf of director Ryusuke Hamaguchi and producer Satoshi Takata from KSFDC Chairman and filmmaker Shaji N Karun

Rajata Chakoram for Best Director

Shokir Kholikov, director of Sunday receiving the Rajata Chakoram for Best Director from KSCA Chairman Ranjith

Rajata Chakoram for Best Debut Director

Fazil Razak, director of The Sentence/ Thadavu receiving the Rajata Chakoram for Best Debut Director from KSCA Chairman Ranjith

Audience Poll Award

Director - Producer Fazil Razak, Co- Producer Pramod Dev and the cast and crew of Thadavu / The Sentence receiving the Audience Poll Award of 28th IFFK from KCWB Chairman and Filmmaker Madhupal

FIPRESCI Award for Best International Film

Felipe Carmona, director of Prison in Andes receiving the FIPRESCI Award for the Best Film in International Competition from Adv D Suresh Kumar, District Panchayat President, Thiruvananthapuram

FIPRESCI Award for Best Malayalam Film

Shruthi Sharanyam, director of B 32 MUTHAL 44 VARE receiving the FIPRESCI Award for the Best Malayalam Film of a Debut Director from Adv D Suresh Kumar, District Panchayat President, Thiruvananthapuram

NETPAC Award for the Best Asian Film

Shokir Kholikov, director of Sunday receiving the NETPAC Award for the Best Asian Film in Competition from VK Prasanth MLA

NETPAC Award for the Best Malayalam Film

Anand Ekarshi, director of AATTAM receiving the NETPAC Award for the Best Malayalam Film from VK Prasanth MLA

FFSI KR Mohanan Award for Best Debut Director from India

Uttam Kamati receiving the FFSI KR Mohanan Award for the Best Debut Director from India, from Adv VK Prasanth MLA for Kherwal at 28th IFFK

Jury Prize

Tatiana Graullera, producer of Totem receiving the Jury Prize for Sound Design for All The Silence on behalf of Lilliana Villasenor, Miguel Hernandez and Mario M Cobos from KSCA Vice Chairman Prem Kumar
